# Product Context

## The Problem

India's property portals (99acres, MagicBricks, Housing.com) generate thousands of leads daily per developer. The painful reality:

- 80% of leads never get a response within the first hour
- Lead qualification odds drop 400% if response takes > 5 minutes
- Sales agents can only call ~30 prospects/day (busy with site visits)
- 40% of inquiries come after business hours (7 PM–11 PM)
- Tier 2/3 city leads prefer Hindi/Hinglish — English-only agents can't convert
- By callback time, the prospect has already spoken to 3–4 other brokers

**Example:** A mid-sized Bangalore developer receiving 150 leads/day with 5 agents — each agent needs 30 calls/day while also doing site visits, closings, and paperwork. It doesn't scale.

## Target Personas

| Persona | Role | Pain Point |
|---|---|---|
| **Sales Manager** | Runs 5–10 agent team at a developer firm | Can't respond fast enough; agents waste time on junk leads |
| **Sales Agent** | Makes 20–30 calls/day manually | 60% of time spent on unqualified "just browsing" leads |

## Key Metric

**Site Visit Conversion Rate** — percentage of raw leads that convert to a scheduled site visit.

- Industry average (manual): 5–8%
- Target with AI qualification: 15–20%

## Why This Use Case

1. **New vertical for Bolna** — not in their template library, shows them a market to enter
2. **Indian market fit** — real estate is ₹25L Cr+ market, phone-first culture, multilingual
3. **Uses Bolna's best features** — multilingual, function calling, webhooks, bulk campaigns
4. **Visually compelling** — pipeline/funnel view demos well
5. **Clear outcome metrics** — qualification rate, response time, cost per qualified lead
